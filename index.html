Alongside Evil Martians, I connect with devtool founders and help them succeed. We help 40 early-stage developer-facing startups each year with GTM, developer experience, backend and frontend development, and infra. People know us for our open source and Ruby on Rails work.

I run Dev Propulsion Labs, a podcast about the business of developer tools. I've talked to founders like David Cramer (Sentry), Paul Copplestone (Supabase), David Gomes (Cursor), DHH (Rails), Harjot Gill (CodeRabbit), Sam Lambert (PlanetScale), Michael Grinich (WorkOS), Zeno Rocha (Resend), Jose Valim (Elixir), and many others.

I wrote about how to turn an open source project into a profitable business for TechCrunch, and regularly write on the Martian Chronicles blog.

Outside of work I am a mom, a wife, an advocate for women in tech, SF lover and a Russian Ipanema girl.
